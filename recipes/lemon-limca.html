Lemon Soda

Ingredients

  • 500ml Water
  • 2 Medium Lemons
  • 50g Brown Sugar
  • 4 drops Lemon essence
  • Ice cubes

Preparation

  1. In a jug add the water and mix in Brown sugar
  2. Squeeze the Lemons in the sugar-water mixture
  3. Add Lemon essence and mix
  4. In a glass add Ice cubes and pour in the Lemon Soda
  5. Enjoy!
